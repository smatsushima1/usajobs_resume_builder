USAJobs Resume Converter

1. First, input your resume to be edited in the left box. Bullet points and separate paragrahs can be used, and are encouraged. 2. Click the "Convert Text" button on the bottom of the left box to convert your resume to a blob of text in the right box to be copied and pasted into your USAJobs resume. 3. Your converted resume can still be edited and the character count on the botttom of the right box will update as you type. When finished, you can use the "Copy Text to Clipboard" button to copy all the text to be pasted into your USAJobs resume, and/or one can download the text into a .txt file.
